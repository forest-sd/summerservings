Our Programs

Multiple pathways to ensure every child is fed.

Open Meal Sites

No registration required — any child 18 and under can receive a free meal at our community sites. Breakfast and lunch served Monday through Friday at parks, libraries, churches, and community centers.

Mobile Meals

For communities without a fixed meal site, our refrigerated trucks deliver meals on a regular schedule. Routes are designed to reach the most underserved neighborhoods.

Family Boxes

Weekly distribution of fresh produce, grains, protein, and dairy for families with children. Available at designated pickup locations with no income verification required.

Volunteer

Join hundreds of community volunteers who make Summer Servings possible — from meal prep and serving to driving delivery routes and organizing distribution events.
