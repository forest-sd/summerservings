Nourishing Communities All Summer Long

We believe every child deserves access to nutritious meals, even when school is out. Our summer meal programs bring fresh food to communities across the region.

Our Programs

Our Mission

Summer Servings is dedicated to closing the nutrition gap that affects millions of children when school cafeterias close for the summer. During the school year, free and reduced-price meal programs provide critical nutrition for families in need. When summer arrives, that lifeline disappears. We bridge the gap by organizing community meal sites, mobile feeding programs, and family food boxes that ensure no child goes hungry during the break.

Community Meal Sites

Free nutritious meals served at parks, libraries, and community centers throughout the summer months.

Mobile Feeding

Our mobile units bring meals directly to neighborhoods with limited access to meal sites.

Family Food Boxes

Weekly produce and pantry boxes for families, providing fresh ingredients for healthy home cooking.
